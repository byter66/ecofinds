Return Policy

Thank you for shopping with us! We strive to provide quality second-hand items that are thoroughly inspected for functionality and condition. However, as these products are pre-owned, please review our return policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. Return Eligibility

You may request a return or exchange within 7 days of receiving your order, provided that:

  • The item is in the condition described at the time of purchase.
  • The defect or issue was not caused by misuse, neglect, or normal wear and tear expected from second-hand goods.
  • All tags, accessories, and packaging (if applicable) are returned with the item.

2. Items Not Eligible for Return

  • Items damaged after delivery due to mishandling or accidental drops.
  • Products showing signs of excessive wear inconsistent with their description.
  • Personal or intimate items (where applicable).

3. Return Process

To initiate a return:

  • Contact our support team within 7 days of delivery.
  • Provide photos or descriptions of the issue.
  • Once approved, ship the item back to us within 5 days using the provided return label.

We recommend using tracked shipping to ensure the safe return of your item.

4. Refunds & Exchanges

  • Upon receiving and inspecting the returned item, we will process a refund or offer an exchange.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method and may take 5-7 business days to reflect.
  • In cases where normal wear and tear are present but do not affect functionality, we may not accept a return.

5. Warranty on Second-Hand Items

While we inspect and test every item before dispatch, please note that second-hand products inherently have reduced durability compared to new items.
